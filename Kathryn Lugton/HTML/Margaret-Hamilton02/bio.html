Margaret Hamilton

Bio

Margaret Heafield Hamilton was born on the 17th of August 1936 in Paoli, Indiana and studied mathematics at the University of Michigan and Earlham College. She graduated with a bachelor's degree in 1958 and took a temporary position at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), where she developed weather prediction software for professor Edward N. Lorenz on the Librascope LGP-30 and PDP-1 computers.

Hamilton later became a famous American computer scientist, systems engineer, and business owner.
